Organization

Just like real military units, the TFT8 has Officers and NCOs in various positions and with different responsibilities. Roles and tasks within the unit are assigned based on experience, qualification and commitment. Unit meetings on a regular basis ensure every member gets a chance to discuss recent events and to share his or her feedback and ideas. Good contributions to unit development are being rewarded. Within the TFT8, several sections exist, each with their own designation.

Detachments of the Task Force

160th SOAR Detachment, Callsign "Razor" - Flight Operations 1st Platoon, B Co., 2/75th Rangers; Callsign "Spartan" - Light Infantry As a Special Operations Air Regiment detachment, "Razor" missions include insertion, resupply, and extraction of Infantry, conducting electronic and visual reconnaissance in support of special operations, performing casualty evacuation, providing close air support as well as a direct action rotary-wing presence. Rangers, initially were trained to conduct raids, ambushes, and airfield seizures; are now conducting combat operations at a higher level of sophistication. Focused on small unit tactics, the Rangers also moved into operational realms that were once the sole purview of Tier One Special Operations units.

Training Procedures

Training in the TFT8 is divided into four(4) mandatory stages every member has to complete in order to move on. As you progress through this procedure you will move up in rank upon completion of each step, you will be a PVT by the time you are finished. Experienced cadre members teach all necessary skills from the basics of shooting to the fine details of Special Operations.

Career Opportunities

Every facet of unit operation in TFT8 is dependant on volunteers. From research and design to teaching and including roles like the Executive Officer, the Operations Sergeant or the Junior Intelligence Sergeant. If recruits are found to be qualified for one of these roles, they receive additional training and specialized equipment. Members who show talent for leadership and administration tasks may be picked for leading roles in the detachments or for the company command staff.

Mods and Features

Mods used by the TFT8 include ACE3 for improvements in interaction, medical system and framework, assets for US and Russian forces from RHS: Escalation, cTAB for command and control, Task Force Radio for authentic voice and radio communications, and ASR AI 3 for custom co-op experiences. . These help to create more authentic gameplay and are mandatory for any action on our private server. Our public server however does not require installed mods, it is set up so that they will be accepted and Task Force Radio will work for all those who have it enabled between one and another.
