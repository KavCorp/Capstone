Recruitment

  • Over the age of 16 years old and have respect and maturity.
  • Fluent in English and have a working headset with push to talk.
  • Not a member of another ARMA 3 unit.
  • Able to devote 5 hours a week of in game playtime to our unit.
  • Must run latest version of TS3
  • You have a willingness to listen, follow, learn and lead as part of a team

Remember, we are looking for mature and disciplined players. Kill/death numbers and fancy moves do not count. We are interested in teamwork and dedication from those willing to prove themselves and become a teammate - the door is always open.
